Model Strategy

Built for clear MRI review, not noisy dashboards.

NeuroScope uses a two-path review approach: a fast thermal preview for common image uploads and a MONAI BraTS segmentation workflow for aligned volume MRI studies.

Thermal image preview

Uploaded images and sampled video frames are normalized, analyzed for abnormal intensity patterns, and returned with a focused thermal overlay.

Full-study segmentation

Volume MRI inputs can use the MONAI BraTS bundle to produce tumor segmentation previews from aligned modalities.

Plain review language

Results avoid raw model scores on the page and instead describe the likely review area in natural scan-location wording.

Comprehensive volume path

Clinical caution
