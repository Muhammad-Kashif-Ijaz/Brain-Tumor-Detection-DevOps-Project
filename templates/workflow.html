Review Workflow

A cleaner path from scan upload to clinical review.

The interface is designed around the way a reviewer thinks: compare the source image, inspect the thermal map, then read a short location-based summary.

1
Load the study

Upload an MRI image, sampled video, camera frame, or aligned volume modalities.

2
Compare side by side

The original scan remains visible while the thermal result appears beside it for immediate comparison.

3
Review highlighted regions

Every detected focus area is highlighted on the returned image and described with plain scan-location language.

4
Escalate for medical interpretation

The result is a visual review aid and should be checked by qualified clinicians before any decision.
